What is this?

This software allows users to check in and out of car parking areas. There is no time limit so stay as long as you like, just try your best to remember to check out when you go home. If you do forget you will be automatically checked out at midnight.

What is Express?

The first time you register you will be given a special code which is tied to your name, company and license plate number. Use this code to check in and out really fast so you can get on with your day. If you forget it just check in as usual and if your details are the same as last time you will use your old code. If your details are the same, e.g. a different license number, another will be created.
